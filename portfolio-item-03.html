Title : Commodity Price Prediction

LSTM Model to predict crude oil prices

The project was made as a starting step to discover the best models for price prediction for stock market, commodity prices and Forex prices.

A Stacked LSTM Model that uses real-time data to forecast the crude oil prices with non-linear correlations between data for upcoming days.

Computed an accuracy score of 92.95% while using 4 layers of LSTM.Enhanced mode capability by including non-linear correlations within Bayesian networks, while adding a risk factor metric.
